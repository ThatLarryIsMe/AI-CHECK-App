Factward Browser Extension — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What this extension does

Factward is a browser extension that lets you fact-check text on any webpage. When you use it, the text you choose to verify is sent to the Factward API for analysis. Results are displayed directly in your browser.

Data we collect

  • Email and password — Used solely for authentication when you sign in. Your password is transmitted securely over HTTPS and is never stored in the extension.
  • Session token — A temporary authentication token stored locally in your browser (via chrome.storage.local) to keep you signed in. This is deleted when you sign out.
  • Text you verify — When you click "Scan this page," "Verify," or right-click to verify, the selected or extracted text is sent to https://factward.ai/api/verify for processing. We do not store page content beyond what is needed to generate your fact-check report.

Data we do NOT collect

  • Browsing history
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Personal data beyond your email
  • Any data from pages you do not explicitly choose to scan

How data is used

  • To authenticate your account
  • To process your fact-check requests
  • To display verification results in your browser

We do not sell, share, or use your data for advertising or any unrelated purpose.

Third-party services

The extension communicates only with https://factward.ai. No data is sent to any other third-party service.

Permissions explained

  • storage — Store your session token locally so you stay signed in
  • activeTab — Access the current tab's content when you click "Scan this page"
  • contextMenus — Add the "Verify with Factward" right-click menu option
  • scripting — Extract text from the page and highlight verified claims
  • notifications — Show status notifications for context-menu verifications

Data retention

Your session token is stored locally until you sign out or it expires. Fact-check reports are stored on Factward's servers in association with your account. You can delete your account and data by contacting us.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at factward.ai.
